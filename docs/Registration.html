Terms and Conditions

  • Full registration includes all sessions scheduled on Days 1, 2, and 3, social networking sessions, and the conference banquet.
  • Single Day registration includes all sessions scheduled on that particular day but does not include the conference banquet. To attend the conference banquet, please purchase a separate ticket.
  • Each accepted paper requires at least one Non-Student Full Registration for inclusion in the conference proceedings. If you have more than one accepted paper, each paper requires a separate registration.
  • The early bird fee will close on Monday 13 Oct 2025 (11:59 pm, AEDT).
  • To request an invitation letter, please contact ausdm2025@gmail.com.
  • All registrations are non-refundable. However, transferring a registration to another attendee is permitted with at least 5 working days' notification sent to ausdm2025@gmail.com.
